Wish vs Hope Test


Hint: 

Hope:  To express something is very possible to happen.
Wish: To express something that is impossible (not likely) to happen. (Never use present simple tense for the clause after "wish" ).

  • I __ that I had never met her.

    >hope
    >>wish

  • I __ that he will succeed.

    >>hope
    >wish

  • We __ that more women will decide to join the company.

    >>hope
    >wish

  • He __ Jenny were with him.

    >hoped
    >>wished

  • I __ that all your efforts won't go for naught.

    >>hope
    >wish
